About Me

I graduated with a Bachelor in International Relations and Business Management in 2014. My career began as Collections Specialist and later on as an Account Manager with BMW Financial services. In July of 2019 my family and I relocated to San Diego where I started as a Dealer Relationship Manager for a Subprime lender.

My transition to a new career as a Web Developer started in June 2020 when I began a 12-week coding bootcamp at UCSD Extension. I excelled during the program finding a new passion. I enjoyed collaborating on group projects with my talented peers and witnessing the amazing progress all of us made over the 12 week period. I take great pride in the progress I made in such a short amount of time and would very much enjoy anyone taking a look at some of my projects. Please feel free to contact me with any questions!
